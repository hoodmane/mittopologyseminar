MIT Topology Seminar

Monday, April 3, 2006
Room 2-142, 4:30pm
Bob Bruner will speak on:

Homotopy fixed points of equivariant S-algebras

Abstract: In joint work with John Rognes, we analyze the homotopy fixed point spectral sequence for the homology of an S^1-equivariant S-algebra. As a consequence we get good collapse results for spectral sequences involved in the calculation of the topological cyclic homology of many MU-spectra.
